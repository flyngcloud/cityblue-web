City Blue Print

— A Wichita Print Shop, Since 1932 —
Wichita, Kansas — Est. 1932

Since 1932, City Blue Print has been Wichita's print shop. Through nine and a half decades, three generations, two World Wars, and the rise of digital printing, the work has stayed the same: print well, design with care, ship on time. We do business cards and billboards. We do banners and blueprints. We do paper stock for designers and surveying supplies for engineers. From a single building at 1400 E Waterman, our work goes out to local clients and ships to wherever fulfillment can reach.
