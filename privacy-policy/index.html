Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

City Blue Print, Inc. ("City Blue Print," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit cityblue.com or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly to us, such as your name, email address, phone number, and company name when you submit a print job, contact us, or create an account on our customer portal. We also collect information about your use of our website, such as pages visited and files uploaded.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to process and fulfill your print orders, communicate with you about your jobs and proofs, send status updates and notifications (including via SMS if you opt in), respond to your questions and requests, and improve our services.

SMS Notifications

If you provide your mobile phone number and opt in to SMS notifications during job submission, we may send you automated text messages regarding your job status, proof approvals, and order completion. Message frequency varies based on order activity. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business, provided those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also disclose information when required by law.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Uploaded files are stored securely and are only accessible to authorized City Blue Print staff and the customer who submitted them.
